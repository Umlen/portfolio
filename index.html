About myself

For the last five years, I have been working at a meat processing plant, where I have had a good career: from a PC Operator to a Production Manager. But now I am highly motivated to switch careers and become a front-end developer.

Why did I choose front-end? I like it that front-end development is a combination of design and programming. Also, front-end is a part of a website that users are directly interacting with, and I really enjoy making simple and beautiful things for users' convenience.

Since December 2021, I have completed 3 courses on freeCodeCamp (Responsive Web Design, JavaScript Algorithms and Data Structures, and Front-End Development Libraries). Now I am going through the Frontend Career Path from Scrimba, doing challenges from Frontendmentor, and solving Kata from CodeWars. So, now I have some experience of using HTML + CSS/SCSS, JS and a little React.js.

If you are interested in someone who has great motivation and a strong interest in becoming a front-end developer, but does not have real experience yet, please contact me.
